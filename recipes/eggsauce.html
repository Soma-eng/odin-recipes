eggsauce

Description

this recipe is very healthy because it contain all the six classes of food

Ingredients

  • egg
  • tomato
  • oil
  • onion
  • pepper

Steps

  1. pour in your oil into a pan
  2. put in your diced tomatoes and onions
  3. put in your pepper
  4. put in your whisked egg
  5. wait for five minutes and your meal is ready
